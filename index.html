Ctrl + Y to redo.

Ctrl + X to cut selected items to clipboard.

Ctrl + Shift + Esc to open task manager.

Win + X to open the hidden menu.

Win + Prtscn to take a screenshot.

Win + I to open settings.

Alt + F4 to close app.
